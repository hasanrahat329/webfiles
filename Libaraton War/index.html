Introduction to Bangladesh Liberation War

The Bangladesh Liberation War took place in 1971, marking a pivotal moment in the nation's history. The war was fought for independence from Pakistan and lasted from March 26, 1971, to December 16, 1971. Millions of people participated in the struggle, and countless heroes sacrificed their lives for the freedom of the nation.
